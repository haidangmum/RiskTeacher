• A strategy is composed of a series of rules designed with the intention of obtaining a specific result of the market, in our case, the goal is to earn more than we lose.
  • In general terms, a strategy must have the following components:
    • 1. When to enter the market
    • 2. When to leave the market.
    • 3. What to do when you are winning.
    • 4. What to do when you are losing.
    • 5. What is the maximum admissible risk?
    • 6. What is the size of the operation
  • People study markets and usually focus on markets and specific instruments, to determine the answers to each of these questions and create their own strategies.
