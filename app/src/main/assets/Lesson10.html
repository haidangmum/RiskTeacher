• A robot is a strategy programmed to run automatically with defined time intervals.
  • You can access our robot simply pressing the bottom AUTO in PaperMoney simulator.
  • This robot includes the basic elements of a strategy.
  • It is possible to create other robots based on other different strategies and specialized in different markets.
  • To know if a robot is profitable is recommended to make tests, the main tests are to review the behavior of the robot with real market data passed, this process is called backtest.
  • These tests help you make decisions about the use and configuration of the robot.
  • In settings you can export the information of your operations to make statistical analysis of your results and in this way to be able to make better decisions about your configuration.
