• In the PaperMoney Simulator section, we have a simple simulator to take operations and see the result immediately.
  • You have a basic balance, this is your money.
  • For BUY operations:
  1. Select the operation Size
  2. Press bottom BUY
  3. The system automatically generates a random number between 1 and 99, if your number is bigger than 52, you win and the operation size value is added to your balance.  Otherwise the operation size value will be subtracted from your balance.
  • For SELL operations:
  1. Select the operation Size
  2. Press bottom SELL
  3. The system automatically generates a random number between 1 and 99, if your number is less than 48, you win and the operation size value is added to your balance.  Otherwise the operation size value will be subtracted from your balance.
  • You can make simulated deposits and withdrawals to your PaperMoney Balance in the Settings Section.
