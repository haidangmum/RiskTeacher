• In short term people have a very low probability of predicting the market, then is highly important for you to control risks when you enter to market.
  • This application is made to teach you about how to control your risks using simple strategies in markets.
  • There are different kind of markets like stocks market, foreign exchange, futures, cryptocurrencies and others.
  • These lessons are made to present simple and powerful concepts related with risks and strategies.
  • At the end of the day, the important thing is not to lose, but to earn more than you lose.
  • If you systematically get more than what you lose and you can repeat this periodically obtaining consistent profits, we say that you have reached the consistency, which is the goal of all market traders.
  • In this application we include a robot with a simple strategy that will show you an example of consistency in an automatic strategy
