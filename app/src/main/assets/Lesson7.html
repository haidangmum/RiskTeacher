• Some examples are:
    • StopLoss:  It means to leave form the market and accept lost. It is better to accept a small loss than to expose all your balance.
    • Hedging: Open an operation in the opposite direction when you are losing. Combining this with the change in operating sizes, you can have coverage strategies that allow you to keep losses controlled. This technique is only allowed in some specific markets such as forex.
    • Martingale: Is a technique to increase the operation size in every loss to recover the past loss and obtain a profit. It is recommended to use any kind of break to this technique in order to avoid big losses. Other valid recommendation is to start with very small operation size, compared with balance size.
  • Our robot uses a kind of martingale, implementing the basic recommendations.
